# Humanitarian Donations & Distribution Partner Guidelines

## Overview

Rakta.js accepts public donations via [buymeacoffee.com/rheinsullivan](https://buymeacoffee.com/rheinsullivan). Collected funds serve two key goals:

1. **Rakta.js Infrastructure Maintenance**: Funding server costs, domain renewals, package publishing, and core framework maintenance.
2. **Social & Humanitarian Support**: Providing aid for low-income communities (dhuafa), orphans, foster homes, nursing homes, and humanitarian relief for **Palestine 🇵🇸 (Free Palestine)**.

## Distribution Partner Application Guidelines

We invite registered charities, orphanages, nursing homes, non-profit foundations, and recognized non-governmental organizations (NGOs) to apply as **authorized distribution partners** for the collected funds.

### Partner Eligibility Criteria

1. **Official Legal Status**: Must be a registered non-profit, foundation, or verified humanitarian organization.
2. **Transparency & Accountability**: Willing to provide periodic public distribution reports and proof of delivery.
3. **Target Programs**: Must operate active relief programs for:
   - Orphans and foster care homes.
   - Senior care homes and elderly welfare.
   - Food & essentials distribution for low-income families.
   - Medical and emergency relief for **Palestine 🇵🇸**.

### Application Process

To apply as a distribution partner:

1. Open a new **GitHub Issue** on the official repository at [github.com/RheinSullivan/raktajs](https://github.com/RheinSullivan/raktajs) titled `[Donation Distribution Partner] Organization Name`.
2. Provide the following details:
   - Official organization name.
   - Registration number / legal credentials.
   - Office address & official website/social media links.
   - Proposed relief project overview.
3. The maintainer will review the credentials and coordinate the transfer process with complete public transparency.
